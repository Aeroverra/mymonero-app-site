MyMonero

The simplest way to use Monero, at the sweet spot between security, convenience, and features

🔏 Be Your Own Bank

Transfer funds with ease. Restore your entire wallet with your seed words alone.

MyMonero is safe and secure. The server can't transfer funds on your behalf because your spend key never leaves your computer.

The app runs natively, so you won't have to worry about whether you've been duped by a look-alike phishing website. You can even set your own MyMonero-compatible server to keep your view key private.

⚡ Lightweight & Efficient

We try to pave over the technical complexities of using digital cash, so you can get back to business.

There's no blockchain node to run. The MyMonero infrastructure is optimized to shoulder the CPU and bandwidth overhead of scanning the blockchain — so your device doesn't have to.

🤓 Smart

100% private contacts keep your payment IDs and addresses in order. Emoji help you keep track of who's who. Work seamlessly with emails and domains by taking advantage of MyMonero's deep OpenAlias integration.

All locally saved data is strongly encrypted — including wallet information, transaction history, your address book, and funds requests.

(Coming soon) The app will intelligently match transactions with contacts and funds requests, and automatically synchronize your secure data across devices so that even the server can't decrypt it.

😎 Native Privacy

We're a team of Monero contributors, so your privacy and security are most important. That’s why our apps are entirely open-source on Github.

The app sends the minimum possible data to the server. It has native support for the crypto behind Monero, so you build and sign transactions directly on your device, not on a cloud server. That way, you remain custodial.

But wait, there's more

  • 🎯 Point-of-sale functionality for speedy transfers — generate a QR code for your customer to scan with their phone so they can just hit "Send".
  • 🌐 Use familiar world currencies like USD seamlessly while transacting with Monero under the hood.
  • 📱 Broad platform support for desktop and mobile with downloadable apps for macOS, Windows, Linux (Electron JS), Android (Cordova JS), iOS (native Swift), and web. Native Android app coming soon.
  • 👍 Never fret about losing your transaction history — there's no wallet file to back up.
  • 💸 Low battery, bandwidth, and storage use — great for mobile devices.
  • 🛡 (Coming soon) Connect over Kovri for enhanced privacy.
  • 🙀 Client app code is entirely open-source and auditable. Collaborators welcome.
