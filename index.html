MyMonero

The best way to use the private digital cash Monero, at the sweet spot between security, privacy, convenience, and features

  • Version 1.0 → Github

🔏 Be Your Own Bank

Transfer funds across borders with ease. Your wallet seed words are all that are needed to access your funds.

MyMonero is safe and secure. The server literally cannot withdraw or transfer your funds because your spend key never leaves your computer.

Because the app runs natively, there's no risk of Javascript code injection. You can even set your own MyMonero-compatible server to avoid disclosing your view key to a third party.

⚡ Lightweight & Efficient

We try to bridge the gap across the technical hurdles associated with using digital cash, so you can get back to business.

There's no node you need to run or connect to. The MyMonero server shoulders the CPU and bandwidth overhead of downloading and scanning the Monero blockchain so your device doesn't have to.

🤓 Smart

Use 100% private contacts to keep your payment IDs and addresses in order. Emoji help you keep track of who's who. Deep OpenAlias integration lets you work seamlessly with emails and domains instead of long-winded strings of random characters.

All locally saved data is strongly encrypted — including wallet information, transaction history, your address book, and funds requests.

(Coming soon) The app will intelligently match transactions with contacts and funds requests, and automatically synchronize your secure data across devices so that even the server can't decrypt it.

😎 Native Privacy

As a team of Monero contributors, your privacy & security are our top priority. That’s why the apps and core libraries are entirely open-source on Github.

The app sends the minimum possible data to the server, and has embedded support for the cryptography behind the privacy-first, next-generation digital cash Monero, so the app can build transactions directly on your device, instead of via some third-party server. This way, you remain the true custodian of your funds.

But wait, there's more

  • 😱 Full point-of-sale functionality for merchants — generate QR codes for your customers to scan from their phones so they can just hit "Send".
  • 🍳 Never fret about losing your transaction history — there's no wallet file to back up because the server handles securely storing and scanning for your transactions in the background.
  • 🗺 Broad support for desktop and mobile platforms with downloadable apps for Mac OS, Windows, Linux (Electron JS), Android (Cordova JS), and iOS (native Swift), with a native Android app and next-generation web wallet on the way.
  • ⛅ The maximum in security and privacy for minimal bandwidth and power use — great for mobile devices
  • 🔐 All address book and funds request meta-data is stored locally under strong encryption.
  • 🛡 (Coming soon) Connect over Kovri and Tor to obscure your IP address.
  • 🔥 Our app codebases are entirely open-source, so the development and security communities can hold it accountable and collaborate on improvements — and we have an open-source back-end server on the way.
