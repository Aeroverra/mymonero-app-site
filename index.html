MyMonero

The simplest way to use Monero, at the sweet spot between security, convenience, and features

  • Version 1.0 → Github

⚡ Lightweight & Efficient

We put design first and ironed out the technical details of using digital money, so you can get back to business.

There's no Monero blockchain node to run. Just download the app on any of your devices and let the wallet server do the hard work.

🔏 Be Your Own Bank

Transfer funds with ease. Restore your entire transfer history with your seed words alone.

MyMonero is safe and secure. The server can't withdraw funds on your behalf because your spend key never leaves your computer.

You can download and run the app natively. No more worrying about look-alike phishing websites and man-in-the-middle attacks trying to steal your keys. You can even set your own MyMonero API-compatible server.

🤓 Smart

Use secure Smart Contacts to keep your private addresses, payment IDs, and yes, even OpenAlias emails & domains in order.

😎 Native Privacy

We're a team of Monero contributors, so your privacy and security are most important. That’s why our apps are entirely open-source on Github.

The app sends the minimum possible data to the server, and all local data is stored under encryption.

MyMonero has native support for the crypto behind Monero, so you build and sign transactions directly on your device. Your spend key never gets sent to some third-party cloud server. That way, you can remain custodial.

But wait, there's more…

  • 🎯 Point-of-sale functionality for speedy transfers — generate QR codes so your customers can scan and just hit "Send".
  • 🌐 Use familiar world currencies like USD seamlessly while transacting with Monero under the hood.
  • 📱 Broad platform support with downloadable desktop apps for macOS, Windows, Linux (Electron JS), mobile apps for Android (Cordova) and iOS (Swift), and even a web wallet.
    (Native Android app in progress.)
  • 👍 Never fret about losing your transaction history — there's no wallet file to back up.
  • 💸 Minimal battery, bandwidth, and storage use — perfect for mobile!
  • 🛡 (Coming soon) Connect over Kovri for enhanced privacy.
  • 🙀 Client app code is entirely auditable and open-source.
    Contributors welcome.
